# My Energy Graph (ASCII Representation)

Here is a simple ASCII representation of your daily energy levels:

Energy Scale:
* = Very Low
** = Low
*** = Medium
**** = High
***** = Highest
- = Zero / Cooked

---

**Fajr - 10 AM:**       *****  (Highest energy, ideal for demanding tasks)
**10 AM - 1 PM:**      ****   (High energy after small breakfast/coffee)
**1 PM - Asr:**        ****   (High energy, "second round" after nap)
**After Asr:**         ***    (Medium energy, small boost)
**After Maghrib:**     *      (Very low energy, suitable for small tasks)
**After Isha:**        -      (Cooked, brain no longer functioning)
